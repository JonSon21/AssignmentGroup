One of the best bang-for-buck runners in Nike’s robust 1990s catalog is making a return soon. The Air Ghost Racer, a super-lightweight marathon shoe from 1998, is set to make its return soon at the most opportune time in which welded mesh and leather uppers seem to be the norm for casual wear. During its original run, the shoes were priced at a mere $80 and famed long-distance Mark Nenow supposedly had a hand in the design.
